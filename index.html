About:

the palladium chain project is about creating blockchain-based technologies and smart contracts. linked to precious metals and collectibles because of their authenticity and immutability.

The project brings with it the idea of ​​the possibility of linking technology to a metal, be it collectible or a means of exchange, facilitating its identification and authentication by intelligent means linked to blockchain technology.

technology in which it would not only be in digital form, but in physical form, where palladium coins and other precious metals are manufactured with contract identification device on the blockchain.

A kind of magnetic card reader where it collects the details of a chip inserted in the coin to identify the technological values ​​of the metal, a way of linking metal + technology, thus attributing two types of values ​​to the object,

physical value of metal
+
digital value of technology

how to insert a quantity of bitcoins inside a currency chip where the asset holder can insert a dynamic quantity of cryptoactive assets, thus providing a certain technological value to the object.

however, the part of the project in which I mention the physical means will not count at the beginning of the project, as the budget limit for development is extremely low, but the digital technological part will be manufactured, leaving open the possibility of technological development linked to collectible metals and metals precious.
